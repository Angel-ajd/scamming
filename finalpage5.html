Ryan Montgomery

Ryan Montgomery is an ethical hacker who exposes child predators and works to protect children. An ethical hacker also known as a white hat hacker or penetration tester, is a cybersecurity professional who is authorized to attempt to penetrate computer systems, networks, or applications to discover and address security vulnerabilities. The goal of ethical hacking is to identify and fix potential weaknesses before malicious hackers can exploit them. THE BEEKEEPER

"The Beekeeper" is a thought-provoking drama film that delves into themes of solitude, redemption, and the intricate balance between nature and humanity. The story follows Ibrahim, a middle-aged beekeeper played by Marcello Mastroianni, who leads a quiet and solitary life in the picturesque Greek countryside. Haunted by his past and the memories of lost love, Ibrahim retreats from society and finds solace in the meticulous care of his beehives. His only companion is his mute assistant, an enigmatic young woman named Spyridoula. His troubles begin when a neighbor of his falls victim to a scam that results in her losing all her money. She then kills herself, and he sets out to seek revenge against those who scammed her out of her life
