Techniacal Support Scam

The Most Common Scam

The technical support scam is a type of fraud in which scammers impersonate technical support representatives from legitimate companies, such as Microsoft, Apple, or antivirus software providers. These scammers typically use various deceptive tactics to trick unsuspecting individuals into believing that their computer has been infected with malware or is experiencing technical issues. Scammers may request remote access to the victim's computer under the guise of diagnosing and resolving technical problems. Once they have access, they can install malicious software, steal personal information, or tamper with the computer's settings. In some cases, scammers may attempt to trick victims into providing personal or financial information, such as credit card numbers or login credentials. This information can be used for identity theft, financial fraud, or other malicious purposes.
